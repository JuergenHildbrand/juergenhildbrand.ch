About me

I am a Junior Web-Developer with experience in building projects with Angular and JavaScript as you can find in my public portfolio.

I started my IT journey in Christmas 1994, when I was given my first computer. And since then, this world has fascinated me. At that time, there were limited options in my area. That is why I had decided to go in another direction.

In my twenty years of professional activities, I have worked in several roles from a workshop to a self-employed welder and assembly manager. Based on my past experience as an assembly manager, I am used to achieving goals together as a team. Technology has always accompanied my professional life and now it was time to take up the topic of computers again. Learning programming was therefore the next logical step for me. Thus I learned the basics of WEB development at the Developer Academy (DE).

I loved to create something with my hands. With programming I found a new challenge, now I can create with the keyboard!

As a former extreme athlete (artistic gymnastics, climbing, crossfit…), I know what perseverance means: I never giving up, but also knowing limits.

I am looking forward to building on my experience and starting my career in IT in a junior developer position.
